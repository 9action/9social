9Social - A tool for Facebook.

With 9Social, you only need one app for all your Facebook and Facebook messenger needs.

9Social is a fast, simple and secure app to access you Facebook. Unlike the official Facebook application, 9Social has been optimized to save space on your device, and preserve your battery. With 9Social, you only need one app for all your Facebook and Facebook messenger.

For users with multiple Facebook or families sharing a device, 9Social makes it easy to switch between accounts.

  • Login with multiple accounts
  • Save space on your device
  • Preserve your battery 15% more than facebook app
  • Easily switch between multiple accounts
  • News feed Option: order timeline by Most Recent or Top Stories
  • Facebook Messenger (no need for an extra app)
